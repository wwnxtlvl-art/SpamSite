Got questions?

How do I place an order?

Order directly through the website by filling out the order form.
You can also send a message on our social media page with the following details:
     - Your name
     - Order quantity
     - Preferred date
✅ Your order will be confirmed once your message or form is received

What payment do we accept?

We accept:
    -  GCash
    -  Cash on Delivery (if available)
    - Cash for meet-up or school pick-up

Payment details will be sent after order confirmation.

How much is the shipping fee?

- Shipping fee depends on your location.
- For school pick-up, there is no shipping fee.
- For nearby areas, the delivery fee will be based on the rider’s rate.

Do we offer discounts or promo codes?

Yes! You can sometimes enjoy special discounts for:
      -  Bulk orders
      -  School events
      - Special promotions
Follow our page to stay updated on promos.

Can I pre-order items?

Yes! You’re encouraged to pre-order at least 1 day before your preferred date to make sure your order is available.
    - Stocks are limited each day because your Spam Musubi is freshly made daily.
